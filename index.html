National Post Graduate College

Established in 1974, National Post Graduate College is an autonomous institution with affiliation with University of Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh. Accredited by the NAAC with grade A, National PG College, Lucknow offers 12 courses across different streams such as Arts, Commerce, Science, Management, etc.

Best placement cell

The Placement Cell acts as a platform that bridges the industry and the students of the College. The Cell aids in organizing campus recruitment drives for part-time and full-time job offers across various sectors.

Frequently Asked Questions

There are some frequently asked question that may help you to find your answer about National Post Graduate College.

  • Is National Post Graduate College is certified by NAAA and UGC?

    National Post Graduate College received 'A' grade accreditation by National Assessment and Accreditation Council [NAAC] and recogniged by UGC.

  • National PG College is public or private college?

    National Post Graduate College is an autonomous institution with affiliation with University of Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh.

  • What is the admission process for national PG College?

    The admission process of National PG college is very simple they take an entrance exam and eligibility is 60% in 12th with recognized board. But in the past 2 years due to the pandamic entrance exam are not conducted.

  • What are the courses offered by National Post Graduate College?

    National Post Graduate College offers various Under-graduate, Post-graduate, Advanced Diploma, PG Diploma and Certificate courses namely: B.A., B.Com., B.Com. (Hons.), B.Sc., BBA, BJMC, BCA, B.Voc., M.A., M.Sc., M.Com., M.Voc., Advanced Diploma, PG Diploma and Certificate course.

  • What is the history of National Post Graduate College?

    National Post Graduate College was established in the year 1974 by the then Chief-minister of Uttar Pradesh, Late Chandra Bhanu Gupta. The College offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses in Arts and Commerce streams and under-graduate courses in Science and Computer streams.
